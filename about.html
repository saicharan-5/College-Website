About Our College

Nalanda Degree & PG College , Jagtial is a premier Institute established in 2005. It is located in Jagtial, Telangana. Our college is Affiliated to Satavahana University, Karimnagar. It has been in the forefront for the past 18 years in providing quality technological education of relevance in the State of Telangana. Students can apply for our college through DOST Official Website . Institute offers Degree courses including 3 UG course and 2 PG course. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. Our College is a reputed institute for B.A., BBA., B.Com, B.Sc., M.Sc and M.Com courses. Students can chose from these programs to persue their career in higher studies. Our College present collection of courses like M.Sc., M.Com., B.A., BBA., B.Com, B.Sc. and Humanities & Social Sciences, Accounting & Commerce, Science programs. Nalanda Degree College, Jagtial offers good infrastructure like Labs, Library etc..

Our College is in 1st position under Satavahana University.
