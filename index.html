Welcome to Nalanda Degree & PG College

Nalanda Degree & PG College, Jagtial is a premier Institute established in 2005. It is located in Telangana. Our college is Affiliated to Satavahana University, Karimnagar. It has been in the forefront for the past 18 years in providing quality technological education of relevance in the State of Telangana. Institute offers Degree courses including 3 UG course and 2 PG course. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. Our College is a reputed institute for B.A., BBA., B.Com, B.Sc., M.Sc Maths ancourses. Students can chose from these programs to persue their career in higher studies. Our College present collection of courses like M.Sc Maths., M.Com., B.A., BBA., B.Com, B.Sc. and Humanities & Social Sciences, Accounting & Commerce, Science programs. Nalanda Degree College, Jagtial offers good infrastructure like Labs, Library etc..
